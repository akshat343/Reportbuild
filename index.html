BENEFITS OF ECOCLEAN
By using ECOCLEAN, you will experience the following benefits in your plant:
  • By eliminating blowdown from your cooling towers, ECOCLEAN will deliver significant reduction in water consumption of your plant
  • As you can directly use raw water in place of treated water (softened/RO/DM water), you will save the treatment cost involved in these processes
  • Since no water will now go from the cooling tower to the ETP for treatment, you will save the cost of ETP treatment
  • Due to improved performance of your cooling towers, the chillers will begin to consume less energy
  • After dosing ECOCLEAN, you will see live micro-organisms (green) die (turn black) within a month
  • The dissolved oxygen will be converted to carbon dioxide and oxides of nitrogen. This will reduce rusting in the system
  • TDS will increase slightly in the beginning as scaling on the surface of the pipes begin to come into the system. The TDS will eventually stabilize to an acceptable level over the course of the trial and continue to remain stable after it.
  • pH of your system will remain almost the same
  • The circulation water will turn transparent at the end of the trial period and continue to remain so after the trial as well
